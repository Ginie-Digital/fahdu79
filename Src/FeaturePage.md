# Feature: Teaser Mode

**Teaser Mode** is a monetization strategy designed to increase subscription conversion rates by leveraging "Fear Of Missing Out" (FOMO).

### Concept
Instead of hiding premium content completely, we display it in the public feed but in a "Locked" state.

### Key Behaviors
1.  **Visibility**: The post appears in the regular feed (Reels/Grid).
2.  **Visual State**:
    *   **Blurred Preview**: The video or image is heavily blurred (Gaussian Blur).
    *   **Lock Overlay**: A prominent Lock icon and "Subscribe to Watch" text overlay the content.
3.  **Interaction**:
    *   Tapping anywhere on the blurred content triggers the **Subscription Bottom Sheet** or **Payment Page**.
    *   Previews (first 3 seconds) can optionally play before locking (if supported).

### Goal
To give non-subscribers a taste of the volume and quality of premium content, prompting them to unlock access immediately.
